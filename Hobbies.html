I am an Aquarist


I love fish keeping. Out of all my hobbies, this is probably my favourate one. I like to maintain ecosystems and keep snails, shrimps, live plants, lots of fish and even a crayfish! I help others et up their fish tanks and even earn money through aquascaping contracts and selling aquatic plants. I breed a species of snail known as 'Assasin snail'. I have also kept an ant colony in a terrarium once. I have read a lot about keeping these creatures and even plan to breed fish in the near future. If you need any advice about keeping exotic fish, insects,reptiles or amphibians, don't shy away! Ask me!
